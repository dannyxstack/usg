Melbourne

Melbourne offers a world-class education, consistently ranked highly globally for liveability and student experience, boasting top universities like UniMelb & Monash, diverse learning opportunities from K-12 to tertiary, and innovative, future-focused schooling with flexible spaces and advanced tech, making it a premier destination for academic growth and skill development in a vibrant, multicultural city.

The campus is located in the heart of the Melbourne Central Business District (CBD). Public transport (trains, trams and buses) is accessible to and from the campus 7 days a week. Penfold facilities boast modern classrooms and computer labs, as well as other training facilities that are ideal for you to gain the most out of your study.

Automotive

Automotive mechanic training involves getting hands-on skills through vocational courses (Cert II, III, IV) at our college and often completing a formal apprenticeship, learning to service, diagnose, and repair vehicles, including new electric/hybrid systems, covering mechanical, electrical, and body repair. Training progresses from basic servicing (Cert II) to advanced diagnosis (Cert IV) and even management (Diploma), with industry-standard ASE certifications proving expertise.
