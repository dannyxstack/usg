Automotive

Automotive mechanic training involves getting hands-on skills through vocational courses (Cert II, III, IV) at our college and often completing a formal apprenticeship, learning to service, diagnose, and repair vehicles, including new electric/hybrid systems, covering mechanical, electrical, and body repair. Training progresses from basic servicing (Cert II) to advanced diagnosis (Cert IV) and even management (Diploma), with industry-standard ASE certifications proving expertise.
